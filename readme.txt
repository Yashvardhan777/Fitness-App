Fithub:
Fithub is a web app made to cater to the needs of any person who wants to begin his/her journey towards a fitter self.
It involves various exercises, both full-body and muscle-specific. The exercises are arranged in the form of a circuit
that can be repeated, as per the user. For those who are looking for a fresh start in this lockdown, this web app is 
the perfect place for them. Simple exercises along with links to guides, helping them out in their fitness journey. 
It also includes motivational videos and quotes when one feels a lack of motivation. The web app uses the MEN stack.
Plus, there is an easter egg waiting for you in one of the web pages.
